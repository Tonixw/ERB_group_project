About Us

Everbloom Florals HK Limited (hereafter 'Everbloom Florals') is more than floral, her desire is to clothe people's feelings, emotions and lives. To be young, to embrace diversity and to savor the moment, that's the spirit that inspires the birth of Everbloom Florals' newest lifestyle concept: Everbloom Florals which combines the three great passions of flower, floral accessories and services, our new brand hopes to whisk customers away on a joyous Hong Kong voyage. We offer a wide range of bouquets & baskets, greenery and floral accessories, that are all perfect for heartfelt expressions at any occasions.
